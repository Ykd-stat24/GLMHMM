====================================================================================================
STATISTICAL METHODS REFERENCE:
====================================================================================================

Kruskal-Wallis H-test:
  Non-parametric alternative to one-way ANOVA testing whether k independent
  samples come from the same distribution. Null hypothesis: all groups have
  identical distributions.

Mann-Whitney U test:
  Non-parametric test comparing two independent samples. Tests whether one
  distribution is stochastically larger than the other.

Effect size (r):
  Standardized measure of difference magnitude independent of sample size.
  Calculated as: r = |Z| / sqrt(N), where Z is standardized U statistic.
